Popular

Gali Ali Beg Waterfall

📍Khalifan

One of Kurdistan's most spectacular natural wonders, Gali Ali Beg features a magnificent waterfall cascading through a narrow gorge surrounded by towering limestone mountains. The site offers breathtaking views, hiking opportunities, and a refreshing escape into nature.

Waterfall Canyon Hiking Photography
Must Visit

Bekhal Waterfall

📍 Rawanduz

A stunning waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and mountains. The crystal-clear water falls from significant heights into natural pools below. Popular for picnics, swimming, and enjoying the serene natural environment. Best visited in spring and early summer.

Waterfall Swimming Picnic Nature

Rawanduz Canyon

📍 Rawanduz

A dramatic deep gorge with steep rocky walls carved by the Great Zab river. The canyon offers spectacular panoramic views and serves as a gateway to many natural attractions. The historic town of Rawanduz perches dramatically on the canyon's edge.

Canyon Photography Scenic Views Historic Town
Historic

Shanidar Cave

📍 Bradost Mountain, Barzan

An archaeological treasure where Neanderthal remains dating back 60,000 years were discovered. This historic cave offers insights into ancient human civilization and features stunning mountain views. A must-visit for history and archaeology enthusiasts.

Cave Archaeology History Mountain Views
Highest Peak

Halgurd Mountain

📍 Choman

Standing at 3,607 meters, Halgurd is the highest peak in Iraq and offers challenging trekking opportunities. Snow-capped for much of the year, it provides spectacular alpine scenery and panoramic views. Perfect for experienced hikers and mountaineers.

Mountain Trekking Snow Sports Alpine

Choman

📍 Choman, Erbil Governorate

A pristine mountainous region known for its natural beauty, traditional villages, and outdoor activities. Features stunning valleys, rivers, and forests. Popular for camping, hiking, and experiencing authentic Kurdish mountain culture.

Mountains Camping Villages Nature

Shinglbana

📍 Rawanduz,

A beautiful mountain resort offering modern amenities amid natural splendor. Features restaurants, chalets, and recreational facilities. Surrounded by forests and mountains, it's perfect for family vacations and weekend getaways.

Resort Accommodation Family-Friendly Recreation

Dukan Lake

📍 Sulaymaniyah Governorate

Kurdistan's largest lake, created by the Dukan Dam on the Little Zab river. Surrounded by mountains, it offers boat tours, fishing, water sports, and stunning sunset views. The lakeside town provides accommodations and restaurants.

Lake Boating Fishing Water Sports

Ahmed Awa Waterfall

📍 Halabja, Sulaymaniyah

A picturesque waterfall flowing year-round through a beautiful valley. The area features restaurants, parks, and walking paths. Known for its refreshing atmosphere and scenic beauty, making it a popular destination for families and nature lovers.

Waterfall Parks Dining Family-Friendly
Adventure

Korek Mountain Resort

📍 Rawanduz, Talafric

Home to the Middle East's longest zipline and cable car system. This premier mountain resort offers breathtaking views, adventure activities, restaurants, and hotels. Features paragliding, skiing in winter, and year-round entertainment.

Cable Car Zipline Resort Adventure

Shaqlawa

📍 Erbil Governorate

A charming mountain town known for its pleasant climate, vineyards, and fruit orchards. Famous for its traditional bazaar, fresh produce, and cool summer weather. Popular weekend destination with numerous parks and restaurants.

Town Bazaar Culture Food

Soran

📍 Erbil Governorate

A historic town surrounded by beautiful mountains and valleys. Gateway to numerous natural attractions including waterfalls and hiking trails. Known for its pleasant climate and traditional Kurdish atmosphere.

Town Gateway Hiking Culture
Ancient

Amedi

📍 Dohuk Governorate

An ancient hilltop city with over 5,000 years of history, perched dramatically on a flat-topped mountain. Features ancient gates, historic buildings, and stunning panoramic views. A unique blend of history and natural beauty.

Ancient City History Architecture Views
Sacred

Lalish

📍 Shekhan, Dohuk Governorate

The holiest temple of the Yazidi faith, nestled in a peaceful valley. Features unique conical spires, sacred springs, and beautiful architecture. A place of great spiritual and cultural significance with serene natural surroundings.

Temple Sacred Site Culture Architecture

Akre

📍 Dohuk Governorate

A picturesque mountain town built on steep slopes, known for its traditional architecture and winding streets. Offers stunning views of surrounding valleys and mountains. Famous for its cool climate and welcoming atmosphere.

Mountain Town Traditional Scenic Culture

Travel Tips

🗓️

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) and Fall (September-November) offer the best weather. Summer is hot in valleys but pleasant in mountains.

🚗

Getting Around

Car rental is recommended for flexibility. Many destinations are accessible by organized tours or private drivers.

👕

What to Pack

Comfortable hiking shoes, layers for varying temperatures, sun protection, and a camera to capture the stunning scenery.

💡

Local Advice

Respect local customs, try traditional Kurdish cuisine, and don't hesitate to interact with friendly locals.
